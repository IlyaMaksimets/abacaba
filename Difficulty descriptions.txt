Beginner difficulty (I'm Too Young to Die):
- Guaranteed drop from every enemy
- 3 additional lifes in every attempt
- Slow turrets and bullets

Medium difficulty (Hurt Me Plenty):
- 70% chance of drop from every enemy
- 2 additional lifes in every attempt
- Normal reaction of turrets and bullets speed

Hard difficulty (Ultra Violence):
- 30% chance of drop from every enemy
- 1 additional life in every attempt
- Very fast reaction of turrets and bullets speed

Insane difficulty (Nightmare):
- No drop from enemies
- Additional lifes is absent
- Perfect reaction of turrets and bullets speed
- No hope...
